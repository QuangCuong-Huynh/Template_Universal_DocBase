# Privacy Impact Assessment (DPIA)

**Domain:** `GRC` &nbsp; **Document ID Pattern:** `GRC-[PROJECT]-DPIA-[DATE]-01`

> GDPR Article 35 assessment: describes processing activity, assesses necessity and proportionality, identifies and mitigates privacy risks, evaluates data subject rights implementation, and records DPO consultation.

## Contents

1. Document Control
2. Processing Activity Description (Nature, Controller, Data Subjects, Categories, Special Category, Purpose, Legal Basis, Volumes, Geography)
3. Necessity & Proportionality Assessment (5 questions)
4. Risk Identification & Assessment (Privacy Risk table with Likelihood, Severity, Score, Mitigation)
5. Data Subject Rights Implementation (Art. 15-21 table: Right, Supported?, Implementation)
6. DPO Consultation & Sign-Off

**GDPR Art. 36:** If high residual risk remains, supervisory authority consultation is mandatory before processing begins.
